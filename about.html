About Secret Message

I created Secret Message as a simple way to share secrets, like passwords, over insecure channels.
Over the years I have seen passwords being send over e-mail, ICQ, Messenger, Skype, SMS, Slack, you name it! I even did it a couple of times myself...

Secret Message is a simple solution to this small but recurring problem.
If you're interested, you can read more about why this is a problem on the FAQ and security page.
