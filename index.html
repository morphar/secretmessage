Secret Message beta

Secret Message is a safer way to share passwords and secrets with trusted receivers over insecure channels.

It is NOT a replacement for good security practices or password managers.


  Write your secret and choose a pass phrase or password that is easy to communicate.

Be sure to communicate the pass phrase safely - preferably face to face.
WARNING: Having the link and the pass phrase will forever enable decryption of the secret.
